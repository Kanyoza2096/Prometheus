Expected Result
The final application should feel like an enterprise AI operating system rather than a simple dashboard.
An administrator should be able to manage an entire ecosystem—including AI services, MIS platforms, plugins, automation workflows, content publishing, integrations, analytics, and infrastructure—from a single interface.
The frontend should be clean enough for a first-time user to navigate easily, yet powerful enough to support enterprise-scale operations with multiple organizations, multiple AI agents, and dozens of connected systems. It should be designed to remain maintainable and extensible as the platform grows over the coming years.
